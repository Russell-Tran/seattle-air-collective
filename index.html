Our Projects & Schemes

Electric Stove Utopia

Every home in America needs to replace its fossil fuel-burning appliances.

Plug The Leak

Municipal natural gas lines or abandoned wells. With surgical precision you lobby the cities in America that are leaking the most methane to fix their pipes; save them money. Alternatively, grow the current volunteer community of people who spend their free time tracking down abandoned oil wells to have them plugged and stop leaking methane.

Fish Tanks 4 Climate

Building a citizen science network of marine aquarium hobbyists who can contribute their tanks to climate change solutions research e.g. seaweed for reduction of cow methane or biofuel

Rice Research Network

Building a network of rice farms who are willing to volunteer some of their paddies for climate change research, either in methane and nitrous oxide mitigation, or general ecology, plant breeding, etc.
